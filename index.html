About Me

Some Fun Facts About Me

Here are some must-know things about me!

  • Student

    I am a second-year computer science major at UCLA. Notable classes taken: Data Structures and Algorithms, Probability, Linear Algebra, Computer Organization, Discrete Math

  • Leader

    I am the ACM Impact Director, where we host workshops and conduct research on tech ethics to spread awareness to the UCLA student community. I am also an ACM AI officer, where I host a podcast "You Belong in AI" with esteemed guests in the artificial intelligence space.

  • Researcher

    I am involved with the ScAi lab and am currently working on a research project on the effects of randomness on fairness in graph neural networks. I also conducted research under the mentorship of MIT professor Julian Shun on fairness in social media recommendation systems. Notable publications: ACM SIGKDD conference, UCLA Undergraduate Journal, Top 300 Regeneron Scholar.

  • Hacker

    I participated in Stanford's Tree Hacks this past February. Along with my team, we built ChatTeach , a virtual learning platform that adapts different characters from movies/TV shows to teach kids educational content, making their learning experience more engaging and effective.

  • Singer

    I love to sing. I have performed at Carnegie Hall, won numerous competitions, and have a singing channel. I also started a nonprofit, Singing for Smiles, during the pandemic to spread joy to whoever needed it most through the power of song.

  • Tennis Enthusiast

    I am part of the UCLA Club tennis team, and am an avid fan of anything tennis. Roger Federer fan for life :)
